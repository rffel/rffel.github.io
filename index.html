About us

Raah Foundation For Enlightening Lives is a not-for-profit organization, which came into existence in June 2017 with an idea of ensuring gender inclusiveness, creating equitable society with special focus on engaging men as a key component to ensure women empowerment thus working to uplift the society as a whole.

The foundation is working with 5E principle which is Education, Entrepreneurship, Environment, Economic Empowerment and Equity. It framed its activities and programs following the 5E principle in accordance to the needs and requirements of the society.
