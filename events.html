Mann ka Dabba campaign

Menstrual Hygiene Day is a day meant to create awareness & to highlight the importance of Menstrual Hygiene & its management.Menstrual Hygiene even today is a subject of shame, associated with enormous taboos, myths. We understand this issue is not glamorous, yet it also makes people uncomfortable to talk about it, but at the same time we somehow feel topics like these are critical to move the needle on women’s rights & empowerment.

Hence on Menstrual Hygiene Day we have launched #MannkaDabba (Thoughts from heart) campaign, wherein women from rural and urban settlements can come forward & share their challenges, pain-points, their everyday life stories & queries pertaining to the subject & the queries were addressed by Dr. Bandana Sodhi – Gynaecologist and their In-house Health expert.We conduct these sessions throughout the year, these sessions primarily highlights the importance of Menstrual health management, Dietary intake, usage of environment friendly products such as Menstrual cups & training on stitching hand-made reusable pads.

We talk about the myths, Taboos, pressures, lack of awareness that exists around this subject. We also discuss the menstrual product deprivation & mechanisms to devise such issues.Simply put, we can't let our girls believe, that onset of menstruation is the end of girls potential.This campaign named as #MANNKADABBA has been inaugurated on 28th may 2019, any women, girl having any question can step it & get their issues addressed by the health experts.

Indie Bazar

Raah Foundation for Enlightening Lives Hosted "Indie Bazaar" on 12th- 13th October 2019 at Italian Embassy Cultural Centre.This was an opportunity to showcase and sell the products/services and exhibit knowledge about green and ethical initiatives in respective fields.The focus of the event was to create awareness and broaden the scope of Ethical consumption. This year Indie Bazaar was styled in a flea market style setting and was dedicated to bringing a host of brands and entrepreneurs under one roof.It was a big success, with over 70 different Brands, with a footfall of over 2200 people, and we have crossed a cumulative sale of 40 Lakhs.We were also covered by the top-notch social media agencies and digital influencers.
