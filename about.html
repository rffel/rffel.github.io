Who are we?

Raah foundation for enlightening lives is a Not for Profit organization that came into existence in June 2017 with an idea of Creating equitable society with special focus on Engaging Men as Key component to Ensure Women Empowerment.Following the concepts of 5E’s and framed its programs in accordance with the needs of the community, following these E’ principles.

Education, Entrepreneurship, Environment, Economic empowerment, Equality are the core areas.

Achieving our core areas by Encouraging & Engaging the vulnerable communities in order to understand deeper issues & tap the untapped areas to ameliorate the pain-points of the grassroots

We aim to provide equal opportunities and access to information, education and employment opportunities through both the perspective and design our programs in such a manner that it can serve the mission of the organization and hence ensure both men and women are enjoying equal rights towards creating peaceful society.

Our Values

We at Raah Foundation for Enlightening Lives believe that without active participation of men it is very difficult to achieve the goal of women empowerment. So we engage men in all our programs and treat them as a very important component. The idea is to create awareness among all the stakeholders about the necessity of inclusion of women in the society at all levels.

Our Mission

Its mission is to empower youth, women and men through relevant education and skill development training. The foundation is continuously working towards bringing positive change in their lives through entrepreneurship development programs and mentorship and also linking them to social security schemes of the government. The idea is to give them hand holding support throughout their journey.

The foundation’s work is targeted for promotion of amicable social behaviour especially to young generation people by actively trying to engage,encourage and educate them.

Our Vision

Raah Foundation for Enlightening Lives is working with a vision to create an equitable society where men and women find life opportunities in a similar manner. The foundation envisions to engage men proactively for ensuring women empowerment thus want to bring catalytic change in the society.
