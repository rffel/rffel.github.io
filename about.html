Who are we?

Raah foundation for enlightening lives is a Not for Profit organization that came into existence in June 2017 with an idea of Creating equitable society with special focus on Engaging Men as Key component to Ensure Women Empowerment.Following the concepts of 5E’s and framed its programs in accordance with the needs of the community, following these E’ principles.

Education, Entrepreneurship, Environment, Economic empowerment, Equality are the core areas.

Achieving our core areas by Encouraging & Engaging the vulnerable communities in order to understand deeper issues & tap the untapped areas to ameliorate the pain-points of the grassroots
